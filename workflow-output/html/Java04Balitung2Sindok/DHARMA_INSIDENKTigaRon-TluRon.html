    Apparatus

    (ca) caṇḍārcciṣ(au)• or read bha caṇḍārcciṣo?— śivāstra• this name is rather hard to recognize on the stone, but the reading is supported by numerous passages where Śivāstra figures as paməgat Tiruan.— masam(ū)n·• the vocalization is rather unclear, but has the broad aspect that would agree better with -ū than with the expected -u.— parbhaktyanya• the reading is at first unclear, but supported by Pintang Mas, l. 3.— kinonya• we presume this is an error for kinon· or (less likely) kinon ta. — amvakanya• understand ambəkanya.— ma ◇ mā

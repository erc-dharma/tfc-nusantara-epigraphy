    Lurah (E. 14)

    Metadata

    Current Version:  draft, 2022-12-24Z

    Editor:   Wayan Jarrah Sastrawan.

    DHARMA Identifier: INSIDENKLurah

    Hands:

    Additional Metadata

    No metadata were provided in the table for this inscription

    Edition

    Recto

    r1n· duruṁ (su)cu vka si tuṇḍan·, tan paliṁ vadhana-gulunyan· dulu(r·) yan· vvaṁ kanalan·
    r2liṁ patana sira tuṇḍan‚ ⟨⟨sa⟩⟩vaḥ tan panaluran a? aya hana ṅatu paruṁ kunalan· vvaṁ
    r3kanalan kuluranaḍaḍahanira tan pa◯⟨⟨na⟩⟩ sira prihambak· sakehira ṅapit iṁ bruha
    r4(m)ava yan ana lu(m/h)atan(·/a) pit· patahun· tanātahun kaḍalan·

    Verso [plate number 2, in the left margin between lines 1 and 2]

    2v1 si(ra), ṅuni-ṅuni yan luṅha ra(b)i tan padesa sira muva⟨ḥ⟩ saṁ kuna(ṁ) yan ana sira lu-
    2v2ṅha ḍi(va)l (sa)ma ya lu_raḥ, human· ◯ pu tajen ana re, sal⟨v⟩irni(ṁ) gave luraḥ
    2v3 kanalan sira muvaḥ yen ana, sira latuḥ skal· kayi sujita sira satahun· r-
    2v4iṁ adoḥ, ma(ra) sira muliha, Ana sira luṅha taruṁ gahakira sa(ji)i ta sira sotaḥ

    Apparatus

    2r1gulu ◇ hulu Br— 2r1vvaṁ ◇ mvaṁ Br
    2v4ma(ra) ◇ Ana Br— 2v4taruṁ ◇ karuṁ Br

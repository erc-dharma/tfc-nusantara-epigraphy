    Commentary

    An alternative designation for this inscription is Kembang Arum.

    - comma punctuation takes shape of median circle

    - tarung in taling/tarung combination is attached in the manner of p and h even to other consonants (as in no, so, ...)

    - anusvāra sometimes used for phonemic/m/ in the word inuṁ “drink” (3r20, 3v3), but sometimes we find the usual spelling inum· (3v4).

    - pasangan ḍ is sometimes not clearly distinguished from pasangan d, resulting in non-standard pairings with ṇ: e.g. Aṇdabhuvaṇa, paṇdavutan

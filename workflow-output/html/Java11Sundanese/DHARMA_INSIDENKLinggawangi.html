Translation

Glowing 3 qualities 3 fires 3 in a human 1: that was the [year in the] Śaka era that Rumatak was marked off by Bhaṭārī Hyaṅ. Finished.

Commentary

Bibliography

The inscription was first edited by Holle (1877: 586–587, in Javanese script); it was edited again by Saleh Danasasmita (1975) and by Hasan Djafar (1991: 16–18). Re-edited and translated here by Aditia Gunawan & Arlo Griffiths, based on the OD photo.

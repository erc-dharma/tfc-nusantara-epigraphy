Translation

Om. Let there be no obstacle! As follows is the decree of Rahyaṅ Niskala Vastu Kañcana , that came down to Rahyaṅ Niṅrat Kañcana and also to His Highness now at Pakuan Pajajaran.

Do not omit to take care of the inhabitants of Jayagiri and the inhabitants of Sundasembawa.

If there is someone providing them livelihood, do not be eager to overcrowd them with the dasa, calagara, cotton timbang, rice paddies dongdang (taxes).

Also with regard to all the [people at the] mouth of the river: do not be eager to ask them toll. Because those who are loved, as it were, [themselves] love the world, [and are those] who firmly put into practice the divine ordinances.

Bibliography

The plates from Kebantenan were first edited by Holle (186718671867: 559–567, 1872: 367–369); they were re-edited by Pleyte who provided some facsimiles of the plates (Hageman 1867(Pleyte 1911: 163–167, 198–199); and edited again by Boechari (1985-1986: № E.42–E.45, 103–107), who offers some new readings, adopted almost integrally by Hasan Djafar (1991: 10–11). This first Kebantenan plate is re-edited here by Aditia Gunawan & Arlo Griffiths from photos of the plate, kindly made available to us by Terrylia Feisrami.

Commentary

Netscher 1855 read angkana (i.e. Aṅkana or Aṁkana) and interpreted it as ‘his/her sign’. We follow Noorduyn (1976) who read Añana. We found the occurence of añana in the Old-Sundanese religious treatise that seems to be synonim with jñana (skt. jñāna).

Translation

The sacred liṅga of Bingba (or Bimba?).

Commentary

Titi S.N. read biŋba and in her translation she gives “Bingba (= Arca)”, which implies she sees a connection with Sanskrit bimba ‘image’. This raises questions both about the meaning of liṅga, and about the history of usage of the anusvāra/cecak sign, which would probably stand for m (not ŋ).

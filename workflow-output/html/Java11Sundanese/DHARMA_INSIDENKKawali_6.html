Apparatus

1-2 pəR̥tigal· • Previous scholars have read pəR̥tiṁgal, but not anusvāra/cecak is found.

Translation

This is the abode of those who are of stable emotions (atisti rasa). If one resides here, he should not gamble: it will lead to suffering.

Commentary

Titi Surti Nastiti (1996: 26 with n. 12 p. 33) takes aya ma as standing at the end of a sentence, which to our knowledge does not agree with the usage we see in Old Sundanese texts, nor with modern Sundanese language. The resulting translation in any case makes no sense.

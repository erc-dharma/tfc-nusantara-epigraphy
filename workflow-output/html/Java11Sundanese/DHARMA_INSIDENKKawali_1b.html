Translation by Noorduyn (1988)


1It should no be defied
2it should be treated wantonly
3anyone striking it will fall prone
4anyone kicking it will fall to the ground

Commentary

Lateral faces (1 = top, 2 = right, 3 = bottom, 4 = left). Noorduyn has observed that this text is in verse form.

Bibliography

First read by Pleyte (18671911: 197). Our text follows that of Noorduyn (1976 and 1988: 309–310), which we have confirmed by consulting our own photographs. Hasan Djafar (1991: 6) and Titi Surti Nastiti (1996: 1–13) contains the same reading as Noorduyn. Richadiana Kartakusuma (2005: 49) has a different readings from her predecessors.

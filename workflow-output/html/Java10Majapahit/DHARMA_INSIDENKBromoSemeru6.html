Commentary

Bibliography

This stone was probably among the group of sixteen inscribed stones at Pasru Jambe reported by Soekmono (1962). It seems no attempt was made to decipher their contents until the first readings published by Sukarto Atmodjo (1986). These inscriptions were republished in a later survey of the Archaeological Service (1994-1995). Re-edited here by Arlo Griffiths and Marine Schoettel from pictures taken by Nigel Bullough and Arlo Griffiths (2011).

Apparatus

1v1 vuĀ[...] B ◇ vuĀ[ra...] CS • Cohen Stuart’s restitution is not confirmed by any occurrence of vuāra in any inscription that I am aware of, although vuara is common.
1v2 Ikanaṁ ◇ Ikana CS B • The anusvāra is clear enough on the estampage.
1v3 saṁ ḍuryyan· muAṁ saṁ siṅgaḥ CS ◇ san daryyan muan singah B • Besides omitting all diacritics on every one of these words, Boechari entirely omits the second saṁ.

    Kawali 2

    Metadata

    Current Version:  draft, 2023-07-31Z

    Editors:   Aditia Gunawan and Arlo Griffiths.

    DHARMA Identifier: INSIDENKKawali_2

    Hand Description:

    Edition

    2nu ṅəsi I-

    5R̥ta bənəṙ

    Apparatus

    2 nu ṅəsi ◇ nu jəḥ F; nu ṅə:si H N HD TSN RK — 2–3 I/ña N HD TSN RK ◇ bhagya F H
    3–4 I/ni N HD TSN ◇ bari F H; bani RK

    Translation by Aditia Gunawan and Arlo Griffiths

    If [you] occupies this (place called) Kawali, lbe observant of the proper behavior, so that [you] remains upright in battle.

    Commentary

    6nañjər ◇ Cf. Pituturniṅ Janma 126: dəgdəg tañjər jaya praṅ ‘indeed upright, victorious in battle’.

    7na ◇ Cf. ḍi na in Kawali 1a line 10 and our commentary to that inscription.

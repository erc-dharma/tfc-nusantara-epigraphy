Apparatus

14r1 /na • The word is likely to have been maṅkana.
14r2 ¿baye?⟨byaya⟩ • The text elsewhere has byaya (14rv1) or byāya (14r5). Cf. also Kurungan, line 4: piṇḍa pirak vyaya.
14r3 samāṅanti⟨ḥ⟩ ◇ samāṅanti vN — 14r3 (v)alaghāra ◇ calaghāra vN
14v1 pūjā-⟨ja⟩pa bisuvāṅkə: n· ◇ pūjā pabisurāṅkə: n· vN • Zoetmulder 1982: s.v. biṣuva, pabiṣuva noted that van Naerssen’s r had to be read v, but did not see that the sequence pūjāpa is a haplographic error for pūjā-japa, as found in Air Asih r5 and Parablyan r4.
14v6 svabhăva ◇ svabhāva vN
14v8 maṁriñca ◇ maṁriñc¿a?⟨i⟩ vN • It is unnecessary to make the correction to maṁriñci proposed by van Naerssen, as the spellings maṁriñca and maṁriñci are both about equally well attested.

Commentary

14v2kanyā(g)ata: one might prima facie prefer the reading kanyāśata, but comparison with Ślokāntara 2 certifies that kanyāgata is intended.
